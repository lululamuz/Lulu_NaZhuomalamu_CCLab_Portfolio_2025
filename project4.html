Exquisite Corpse

Project 4 · p5.js · Collaboration · Surrealist technique

p5.js Sketch

Description

Project 4: Exquisite Corpse. This assignment explores the surrealist technique of exquisite corpse, where players take turns drawing portions of a figure without seeing what has already been drawn by others.

The final image is revealed at the end, often creating surprising and unexpected forms and narratives. In this piece, the head is my original design, while the body and lower portion were contributed by classmates.
